About AfriTutor

Our Vision

To bridge the education gap in Africa by providing every learner with access to quality, AI-powered educational resources in their own language, even offline.

Our Mission

AfriTutor empowers African learners with AI-powered educational tools that work in multiple languages and low-data environments. We believe every student deserves access to quality learning, no matter where they are.

Why AfriTutor?

  • Designed for African students, teachers, and parents
  • Works offline and on low-bandwidth connections
  • Supports local languages and cultures
  • Built by a passionate, diverse team
